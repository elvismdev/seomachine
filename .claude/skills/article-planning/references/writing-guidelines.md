# Article Writing Guidelines

## Section Types and Targets

| Section Type | Word Target | Key Elements |
|-------------|-------------|--------------|
| Introduction | 150-200 | Hook, context, promise |
| How-To Body | 300-500 | Steps, examples, tips |
| Comparison Body | 300-500 | Criteria, pros/cons, verdict |
| FAQ | 100-200 | Question, concise answer |
| Conclusion | 150-200 | Summary, CTA, next steps |

## CTA Placement Strategy

| Position | CTA Type | Example |
|----------|----------|---------|
| After intro (20%) | Soft CTA | "Start your free trial" |
| Mid-article (50%) | Contextual CTA | "See how [product] handles this" |
| Conclusion (90%) | Strong CTA | "Get started today" |

## AI Phrases to Avoid

These phrases are flagged by the content quality scorer:
- "In today's digital landscape"
- "Let's dive in"
- "It's important to note"
- "In this comprehensive guide"
- "Game-changer"
- "Leverage"
- "Robust"

Use natural, conversational alternatives instead.
