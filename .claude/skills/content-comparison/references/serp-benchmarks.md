# SERP Content Length Benchmarks

## By Search Intent

| Intent | Median Length | 75th Percentile | Recommended |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Informational (how-to) | 2,000 | 2,800 | 2,000-3,000 |
| Commercial (comparison) | 2,500 | 3,500 | 2,500-3,500 |
| Transactional (product) | 1,200 | 1,800 | 1,000-2,000 |
| Navigational | 800 | 1,200 | 500-1,500 |

## Content Depth Indicators

Beyond word count, competitive content typically includes:
- 5-8 H2 sections (with keyword variants)
- 2-4 data points or statistics per section
- At least 1 table or comparison chart
- 3-5 internal links and 2-3 external citations
- FAQ section with 5-8 questions
